About

I am a computer scientist currently pursuing my PhD in Machine Learning and working as a research associate in the Institute of Informatics and Telecommunications in NCSR Demokritos.

You can get a pdf of my detailed cv in English (Updated 3-2021).

Alternatively, check out a tl;dr version below.

Education

  • PhD in machine learning, from the Informatics and Telecommunications department (DIT) of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens (NKUA), co-supervised by the Institute of Informatics and Telecommunications (IIT) of the National Center for Scientific Research Demokritos (NCSRD), funded by the St. Niarchos 2017 Industrial PhD scholarships (in progress).

  • MSc in Signal and Information Processing and Learning from DIT of the NKUA. My thesis was Multimodal video classification with deep neural networks.

  • BSc in Computer Science from the Computer Science and Engineering department of the University of Ioannina, where my thesis was titled Image registration with unified particle swarm optimization.

Employment

  • Machine Learning Engineer at SciFY, working on classification methods of audio transcriptions in the law domain (current).
  • Research associate at the ATHENA Research Center, where I worked on topic modelling and data analysis of biomedical scientific publications.
  • Research associate at the Software and Knowledge Engineering Laboratory, of the IIT at the NCSRD, where I worked on event detection, big textual data and graph methods for automatic summarization.
  • Research associate at the Multimedia Knowledge and Social Media Analytics Laboratory, of the Information Technologies Institute at the Centre for Research and Technology Hellas, where I worked on video concept detection, specifically visual descriptors, classification models and deep neural networks.

Research

My main research focuses on:

  • Machine learning in text and multimedia
  • Neural networks and deep learning
  • Automatic summarization
