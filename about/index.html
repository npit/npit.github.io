About

I am a computer scientist currently employed as a researcher at the Institute of Informatics and Telecommunications of the National Center for Scientific Research Demokritos. I am currently working on event detection from text and news sources, automatic summarization, big data and machine learning.

You can get a pdf of my detailed cv in English and Greek (Updated 09/2017). Alternatively, you can check out a tl;dr version below.

Education

  • MSc in Signal and Information Processing and Learning from the Informatics and Telecommunications department of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. My thesis studies video and image classification and description using deep neural networks (in progress).

  • BSc in Computer Science from the Computer Science and Engineering department of the University of Ioannina, where my thesis was titled Image registration with unified particle swarm optimization and was supervised by Christophoros Nikou.

Employment
